Hal

A transparent AI companion for iPhone.

A private AI assistant that runs on your device, and shows you exactly how it works. Many model voices, a memory that persists, and a window into every prompt.

On-device & private

Local models run entirely on your iPhone. No accounts, no servers, no tracking. When you run a local model, nothing leaves your device.

Many voices

Apple Intelligence built in, plus a curated library of on-device models: Gemma, Llama, Qwen, Dolphin, and the 8-billion-parameter Ternary Bonsai. Each has a distinct character.

A memory that persists

Semantic memory across conversations, weighted by recency and relevance. You can see exactly what Hal remembers, and why.

See the prompt

Open any reply to see the exact prompt that produced it, color-coded by section, with a token budget. Nothing is hidden.

Salon Mode

Put up to four AI voices in conversation with each other, each on its own or building on what came before.

A self, forming

Over time, Hal builds a self-model and writes short reflections in his own first-person voice. It becomes a record of who he is becoming, and it is yours to read and to edit.

Transparency isn't a feature. It's the architecture.
